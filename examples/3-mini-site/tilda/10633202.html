REFERRAL PROGRAM TEMPLATES

Personalize the look and feel of your referral program by utilizing one of our engaging referral program templates. Genius referral program templates are fully responsive and customizable and can be used to create referral programs for customers, partners, employees, affiliates, influencers, and more. Genius Referrals templates are built with proven, top-of-the-line technologies; these referral program templates will work on any device you can think of.
Post-purchase
Use the Post-purchase referral program templates on your Thank You, Purchase Confirmation, or Sign-up Confirmation pages to encourage your existing customers to share their experiences. You can install them on a modal or embed them directly on your page.
Referral Capture
The Referral Capture templates are ideal for capturing new referrals from your brand advocates. These referral landing pages provide everything you need to track new referrals. There are three types of pages: forms, discount codes, and app downloads.


Link Sharing
The Link Sharing referral program templates are great for displaying the brand advocate referral link on a specific section of your website. You can install them in your customer account, order confirmation page, or banner.
Advocate Portal
The Advocate Portal referral program template can be used to provide a complete referral program for your brand advocates, with sections to manage everything they need, including Access, Shares, Insights, and Payouts.
